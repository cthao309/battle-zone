  • How To Play?

    • Battle Zone is a game alterating between two person

    The game goes like this:

    • 1. Start game by clicking on the "Start Game" button.
      2. Players guess the position of the other tanker's position and fire.
      3. If the guessed number is not the correct position of the other tanker, the game will alternate to the other player
      4. This will continue until one of the player guess it correctly or they reset the game
